About Our Program

Reach for Success Program consists of 12 online lessons designed to help students build positive relationships for their school success. Every lesson reflects the RICH 4 Guiding Principles:

  1. I MATTER
  2. I am RESPONSIBLE for my behavior
  3. I am CONSIDERATE of classmates and others
  4. I use THINKING STRATEGIES for school success

Online Activities

  • Homepage spaceship quiz game
  • RICH's 4 Guiding Principles Podcast
  • Interactive projects

RICH Student Zone Portal

  • Web-Based Learning Thematic Projects

Resources for Parents
